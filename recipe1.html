Easy Protein Bar 🥜

Ingredients

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1 scoop protein powder
  • Dark chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix oats, protein powder, and chocolate chips in a bowl.
  2. Add peanut butter and honey.
  3. Mix everything well until it forms a thick mixture.
  4. Press the mixture into a tray evenly.
  5. Refrigerate for 1-2 hours.
  6. Cut into bars and serve.

Preparation Time: 10 minutes + chilling time

Difficulty: Easy
