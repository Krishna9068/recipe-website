Pasta Alfredo 🍝

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta until soft.
  2. Heat butter and sauté garlic.
  3. Add cream and stir well.
  4. Add cheese and mix until smooth.
  5. Add pasta and mix properly.
  6. Serve hot.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Medium
