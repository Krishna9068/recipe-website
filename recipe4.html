Veg Fried Rice 🍚

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • Mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and sauté garlic.
  2. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  3. Add cooked rice and mix well.
  4. Add soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Stir fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Serve hot.

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
