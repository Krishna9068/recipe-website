Shakshuka 🍳

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 small onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and chili flakes.
  4. Make small spaces and crack eggs into the sauce.
  5. Cover and cook until eggs are set.
  6. Garnish with coriander and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 15-20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
