Chocolate Mug Cake 🍫

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a mug.
  2. Add milk and oil and mix well.
  3. Add chocolate chips if you like.
  4. Microwave for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Let it cool slightly and enjoy.

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Difficulty: Easy
